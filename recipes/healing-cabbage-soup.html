Healing Cabbage Soup

Back to Odin Recipes

Description

Healing Cabbage Soup is a healthy and nutritious soup that is perfect for cold winter days.

Ingredients
  • 1/2 head of cabbage
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
Steps
  1. Chop the cabbage, carrots, and celery.
  2. Boil the vegetables in water for 30 minutes.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Enjoy your healing cabbage soup!
