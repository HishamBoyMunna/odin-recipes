Biriyani

Back to Odin Recipes

Description

Biriyani, a spicy chicken and rice dish, is one of the most well-known Indian-Pakistani dishes.

Ingredients
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 lb chicken
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1/4 cup fried onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped green chilies
  • 1/4 cup ginger garlic paste
  • 1/4 cup biriyani masala
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup saffron milk
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup salt
Steps
  1. Wash and soak the rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, milk, ghee, oil, fried onions, mint leaves, coriander leaves, green chilies, ginger garlic paste, biriyani masala, lemon juice, and salt for 1 hour.
  3. Boil the rice with saffron milk and water until 70% cooked.
  4. Layer the chicken and rice in a pot and cook on low heat for 30 minutes.
  5. Enjoy your biriyani!
