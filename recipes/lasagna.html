Lasagna

Back to Odin Recipes

Description

Lasagna, a cheesy pasta dish, is one of the most well-known Italian dishes.

Ingredients
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup onion
  • 1/4 cup garlic
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup dried basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup fennel seeds
  • 1/4 cup Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 cup salt
  • 1/4 cup ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup parsley
  • 1/4 cup lasagna noodles
  • 1/4 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic until brown.
  3. Add the tomato sauce, tomato paste, water, sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper, and parsley.
  4. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. In a large bowl, combine the ricotta cheese with the egg and remaining parsley.
  6. Spread 1 cup of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  7. Layer with uncooked lasagna noodles, ricotta cheese mixture, mozzarella cheese, and meat sauce.
  8. Continue layering until all ingredients are used, reserving 1/2 cup mozzarella.
  9. Cover with foil.
  10. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes.
  12. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.
  13. Enjoy your lasagna!
